NightOut — Support

NightOut (BYN) is a social nightlife app: see which venues are busy tonight, share plans with friends, and report queue times and vibes in real time.

Contact

Questions, bug reports, or feedback: joar.klepp@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

Common questions

  • How do I delete my account? In the app: Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and removes all your data permanently.
  • How do I report or block a user? Open their profile, tap the «…» button, and choose Report or Block. Blocked users can be managed under Settings → Blocked users. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.
  • Why does the app ask for my location? Only to sort venues by distance and to verify you're near a venue before submitting queue/vibe reports. Your GPS position never leaves your device.
  • Who can see my check-ins? Only your accepted friends. Venue counts shown to others are anonymous.
